How Their Points are Converted

Every food restaurant that offers loyalty and rewards has a different structure and rate by which they convert the money you spend into rewards points and redeemables. Below is the conversion used to convert your rival brand’s points points into Daddy’s Chicken Shack® Points. Every attempt was made to make the conversion as equal as possible. 

Chick-Fil-A Points DIVIDED BY 6 = Daddy’s Points

Dave’s Hot Chicken Points DIVIDED By 6 = Daddy’s Points

Slim Chickens Points Multiplied by .6 = Daddy’s Points. Slim Chickens BDAY Dessert = Daddy’s 40 points

Popeyes Points DIVIDED by 5 = Daddy’s Points

KFC Points DIVIDED by 3 = Daddy’s points

40 Daddy’s points = Raising Canes free bday drink
140 Daddy’s Points = Raising Canes BOGO Box on anniversary
